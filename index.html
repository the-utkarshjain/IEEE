IEEE IIT Mandi is a student chapter of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE). We are a student-run organization at the IIT Mandi. We are a group of students who want to have fun and meet new people, to learn about research and innovation in technology, to find ourselves and our passions, to help our community, and to get inspired to change the world!


What is IEEE?

The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers, or IEEE, is the worldâ€™s leading professional association for the advancement of technology involving electricity.
